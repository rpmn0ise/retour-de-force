## Description

BUGS:
-It is a lot slower than IRL to prevent exploding due to Beamng's physics
-Causes beamng instability when it crashes into water
-May bug and cause flashing images
-Control surfaces may become unresponsive or unstable at times
-Some parts of meshes have holes
-Textures dont perfectly align on skins

## Installation

1. Télécharger le fichier `.zip`
2. Ouvrir **BeamNG.drive** → **Repository** (icône carton)
3. Glisser-déposer le `.zip` dans la fenêtre
4. Activer le mod puis redémarrer le jeu

> Compatible BeamNG.drive 0.30+
